INSTRUCTIONS

Teleportation

To navigate and explore the environment: Push joystick forward/backward.
For incremental turn: Push joystick left/right.

Interaction

Look out for the highlighted objects!! You can interact with them.
To interact with an object: Press the trigger button.

Exit

To exit the immersive mode: Press the oculus home button on the controller and click exit.

Once the scene has fully loaded, you will see the Enter-XR UI button on the bottom right of the screen. Press the button to enter the experience.
